School of Management

The School of Management was created in June 1996. The School of Management is located in the most intense business ecosystems which allow them and their students access to management, agribusiness, and other leader that specialize in different areas of management. The School of Management provides a curriculum that is rigorous enough for students to prepare in the modern marketplace. This school brings together students from across the globe.
